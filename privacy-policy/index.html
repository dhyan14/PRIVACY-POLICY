Privacy Policy

Last Updated: TODAY’S DATE

This Privacy Policy describes how the Calculator App ("we", "our", or "us") handles user information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This app includes two features: Standard Calculator and Loan EMI Finance Calculator.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The Calculator App does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Financial data
  • Location data
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Contact list or SMS
  • Any background data

2. Permissions Used

The Calculator App does not request any device permissions. Both the Standard Calculator and Loan EMI Calculator work completely offline without accessing any system features.

3. How Calculations Work

Standard Calculator

All calculations are performed locally on your device. No data is transmitted or stored anywhere.

Loan EMI Finance Calculator

EMI calculations are performed using values entered by the user (loan amount, interest rate, tenure). These inputs are used only for immediate calculation and are not saved or sent to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

The Calculator App does not use any third-party services such as analytics, ads, SDKs, or tracking libraries.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any data from children under the age of 13 or any other users.

6. Security

Since the app does not collect or store any data, there is no risk of data leakage or misuse.
